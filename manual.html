📖 MOOVED Field Sales Manual

How to use the Field Hunter app · For sales team · last updated July 2026

1. Getting Started

📱 Open the App

  1. Open Chrome or Safari on your phone
  2. Go to: https://leo520-dev.github.io/mooved-field-hunter/index.html
  3. Or scan QR code (ask your manager)

👤 Enter Your Name

The top-left box Your name — type your name so the manager knows who you are.

This is saved automatically.

📲 Install on Home Screen (Recommended)

Android (Chrome): Chrome will show Add to Home Screen popup → tap Install

iPhone (Safari): Tap Share button (square+arrow) → Add to Home Screen → Add

⚠️ Installing makes the app work offline and feel like a real app. Highly recommended!

2. Daily Workflow

🎯 Step 1: Choose Your Region

At the top, tap your city: 📍 Accra / 🏛️ Kumasi / 🌊 Takoradi

🔍 Step 2: Find Your Target District

Scroll or use the Search box to find the district you're going to.
Filter by type: 🏡Res 🏢Comm 🔋Solar etc.

📍 Step 3: Mark Your Visit

When you arrive at a district, tap the two buttons on the card:

▼ More — Expand to see strategy, shops to visit, and all tools

○ Visit — Mark the district as visited (this records your GPS location)

⚠️ You MUST allow GPS/location permission when prompted. Without GPS, the manager cannot verify you were there.

📝 Step 4: Write Notes

After expanding the card (▼ More), scroll down to 🎙️ Voice · 📝 Notes section:

  • Type notes about shop owners, pricing, opportunities
  • Tap 💾 Save to save each note
  • You can add multiple notes per district

🎙️ Step 5: Record Voice Memos

If typing is difficult, use voice:

  1. Tap 🎙️ Record
  2. Start speaking (describe the shop, person, deal)
  3. Tap ⏹️ Stop when done
  4. The recording is saved — you can play it back later to write proper notes

📸 Step 6: Take Photos

In the expanded card, scroll to 📸 Photos section:

  1. Tap 📷 Photo
  2. Take a photo of the shop/storefront/customer
  3. Photos are automatically compressed for cloud upload
  4. You can see your photos in the Dashboard later

👤 Step 7: Add Customer Contacts

In the expanded card, find 👤 Contacts section:

  1. Fill in: Name, Phone number
  2. Select interest level: 🔥 Hot / 💛 Warm / ❄️ Cold
  3. Tap + Add

3. Sync & Data

☁️ Automatic Sync

Every time you:

  • Mark a visit (○ Visit)
  • Save a note (💾 Save)
  • Record GPS (📍 GPS button)

Your data is automatically sent to the company database. No manual upload needed.

⚠️ If you see "Synced" at the top status bar, everything is working. If not, check your internet connection.

🗺️ Map View

Tap the 🗺️ Map tab at the top to see all districts on a map.
Visited districts show as colored dots.

📊 Checking Your Progress

The top bar shows:

  • Districts — total districts available
  • Visited — how many you've covered
  • Progress — percentage complete
  • GPS — total GPS check-ins
  • Contacts — total contacts added

4. Tips & Best Practices

  1. 📱 Always enter your name at the top — otherwise the manager won't know who you are
  2. 📍 Allow GPS permission — this is how the company tracks your location
  3. 📝 Write notes immediately while the information is fresh
  4. 📸 Take photos of shop fronts — helps the manager understand the area
  5. ☀️ Best hours: Tuesday-Thursday 9:00-12:00 and 14:00-17:00
  6. 🚗 Use a charter car (GHS 200-300/day) for efficiency
  7. 👔 Wear your MOOVED branded polo for professionalism
  8. 💰 Carry small change (GHS 100) for tips and incentives
